What is Framing

Step 1: Visualize

This is the key that will unlock your inner-designer. Pull out your background paper and a set of our Designer Templates and start moving them around on your page. Mix and match them, layer themâ€¦the possibilities are endless.

Step 2: Trace & Cut

Once you have designed a frame for your pictures, simply trace each design element onto your coordinating paper and cut out with a pair of scissors.

Step 3: Replace

Now replace each template with the design element on your page. You will be amazed at how new designs will spring to life!

Step 4: Cherish

By using this technique, along with our Designer Templates, you will save time, get in touch with the designer in you, and save money by taking advantage of the beautiful patterns on your scrapbook paper to embellish your page!
